What is CVcase?

CVcase is a powerful and growing platform developed for you to find jobs and apply smart.

  • ✔️ Upload your existing CV, or contact us on Telegram to have us build your CV for you.
  • ✔️ Find jobs — Feed Jobs shows every listing, and Smart Finder searches and filters jobs to match your CV automatically.
  • ✔️ Auto letter generation — generate a tailored application letter with one click, for jobs posted on CVcase or for any job posted elsewhere via its link.
  • ✔️ Merge your documents — combine your letter with your CV and any other credentials or certificates required by the job post.
